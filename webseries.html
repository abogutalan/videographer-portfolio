The basement episode 1

Webseries on YoutbeLink   | Perunnal Kootam Channel  

The Basement was a 5 episode mini-series created for social media platforms mainly catering to the YouTube audience. The series belongs to the mockumentary genre, and was targeted towards a younger audience. The main theme revolved around the every day lives of new immigrants and their trivial pursuits. This was one of my passion projects and it came to fruition after I found the right team. The process of making this series included scriptwriting and preparing screenplay in the pre-production phase. Cast were chosen according to the script. The production phase involved, set and art designing,setting camera, light and sound equipment. The post-production phase included a combination of editing, sound mixing and graphic designing. I thoroughly enjoyed creating my own signature content and was proud to call myself a content-creator. The channel has a growing niche audience and managed to gain popularity among immigrants as the stories were relatable and had tongue in cheek humor.
