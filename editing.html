P4E CAREER FAIR




Partnerships for Employment (P4E) is a collaborative effort among the University of Guelph, University of Waterloo, Wilfrid Laurier University and Conestoga College. The partnership was created in 1994 to better serve students, alumni and employers through jointly sponsored annual Career Fairs and Job Fairs. The process involved preparing an interview script, capturing related b-rolls using single camera set up, interviewing guests on camera and audio recording. The editing was done in Adobe Premiere software.
