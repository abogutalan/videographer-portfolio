TITLE OPENER CREDITS

Title opener for a cooking show




Title openers are important in opening a segment, and drawing the attention of viewers is key part of this. Making show opener and title credits is something I enjoy, using After effects software, most of the projects are done using collecting stock images, resizing, applying masking and transitions for the titles and images. The final sound mixing and editing is usually done in Adobe Premiere Pro. Here are some of the title graphics videos I created based on the segment genre demands and treatment. Most of the openers showcased here were created as part of Conestoga College's Television Broadcasting shows produced by the crew.
